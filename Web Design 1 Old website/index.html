Welcome To My Winter Break

Winter, winter, winter it's not my favorite nor do I hate it. It's just that things don't feel the same to me anymore. This past winter, I didn't enjoy it because I didn't do anything other than playing some video games with friends, celebrating my partner's birthday on Christmas Eve, capturing some black and white photographs, drawing, and taking care of my family. I wish I could say I did more.

Meanwhile, as my winter break was dull, I have been managing to do a lot of self-care because having depression makes everything feel so melancholy. I manage to get more sleep, exercise, express myself more and try not to dwell on the bad things in life.

  • Feeling listless, sad or down most of the day, nearly every day
  • Losing interest in activities I once enjoyed
  • Having low energy and feeling sluggish
  • Having problems with sleeping too much
  • Having thoughts of not wanting to live
